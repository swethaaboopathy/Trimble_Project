About the Project

The Trimble Project, in partnership with Learning Links Foundation, is a community-driven initiative focused on strengthening STEM education, empowering girl students, and building an inclusive learning ecosystem through innovation, technology, and community engagement. The project aims to bridge the gender gap in STEM by providing equitable access to hands-on learning, emerging technologies, leadership opportunities, career guidance, and innovation platforms that enhance academic achievement, creativity, critical thinking, and future-ready skills among students.The initiative also promotes women empowerment by supporting mothers through financial literacy, vocational training, enterprise opportunities, market exposure, and access to government schemes and savings initiatives. By fostering strong school–community partnerships, volunteering programs, and sustainable development efforts, the project contributes towards gender equality, social transformation, and long-term community impact.

Key Milestones

January 2023
Project Launch
The STEM Lab under the Trimble Project, in partnership with Learning Links Foundation, was officially launched on September 11, 2023, at Jaigopal Garodia Government Girls Higher Secondary School. The launch marked the beginning of a transformative initiative aimed at empowering girl students through hands-on STEM education, innovation, digital learning, and experiential activities designed to enhance creativity, problem-solving, and future-ready skills.
Since 2023
Transforming Education Through STEM Innovation
The Trimble STEM Lab at Jaigopal Garodia Government Girls Higher Secondary School has achieved several significant milestones in STEM education, student empowerment, and community engagement. The project has positively impacted hundreds of girl students through hands-on STEM learning, innovation activities, advanced project-based sessions, career guidance, scholarship opportunities, and exposure to emerging technologies.
Journey of 2023 - 2026
Project Showcase
Through robotics, coding, 3D design, project-based learning, competitions, scholarships, and leadership opportunities, students have developed creativity, confidence, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities while gaining recognition at district, state, national, and international platforms.The project has also focused on strengthening teacher capacity through STEM and digital upskilling programs and empowering mothers through financial literacy, vocational training, enterprise opportunities, and awareness on government schemes and savings initiatives.By fostering strong school–community partnerships, volunteering activities, and inclusive development initiatives. Year 2023

The year 2023 marked a significant milestone for the Trimble Project as it began its journey towards empowering girls through STEM education and community transformation across Chennai and Mumbai. In partnership with Learning Links Foundation, the initiative created impactful learning opportunities through STEM tinkering sessions, life skills programs, innovation activities, teacher training, and women empowerment initiatives. Students actively participated in national and international competitions, innovation challenges, and exposure visits, while mothers benefited from financial literacy and vocational training programs that promoted economic independence and confidence.The project also strengthened school infrastructure through STEM lab development, classroom enhancements, and volunteering initiatives, creating a more inclusive, innovative, and future-ready learning ecosystem..

Year 2025

The Trimble Project 2025–26 stood out for its remarkable achievements in empowering girls through STEM education, innovation, and community engagement across Chennai and Mumbai. Students actively participated in major competitions such as SIDP 2025, Technova25, InnovaTN, Bebras India Challenge, Young Kalam Science Festival, and Khoj-7 Innovation Competition, winning prizes and earning recognition at district, state, and international levels. The project successfully conducted over 1,100 tinkering sessions along with digital literacy, life skill, and teacher upskilling programs, creating a strong culture of innovation and hands-on learning. Women empowerment initiatives enabled mothers to receive vocational training and showcase their products through enterprise activities, collectively earning nearly ₹20,000 and building entrepreneurial confidence. A major milestone of the year was receiving the “Most Impactful Girl Child Education Programme Initiative of the Year – 2025”award at the Indian CSR Summit & Awards 2025, recognizing the project’s outstanding social impact.
